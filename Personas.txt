Personas

DCI Member Core
	- wants current and reliable information about the Interlaken Flying area
		- by knowing what is open and why (and why other things are closed)
		- by getting informed about current impediments and events in the area
	- wants to feel like a part of a community
		- by flying together with other DCI Members
		- by attending DCI Events 
		- by beeing in Communication Channels for DCI Members and beeing able to discuss ideas with other DCI Members
		- by appearing in the Social Media Feed of DCI (on the page and on the DCI Social Media accounts) 
	- Joined the Club because hes flying so often in Interlaken he doesn't want to pay the landing taxes every time or because he just wants to support DCI
	- Expects the DCI to make sure as many Launch and Landing Sites are available as possible
		- by buying or renting property
		- by Communicating with Stakeholders

DCI Member Newbie
	- wants current and reliable information about the Interlaken Flying area
		- by beeing informed in detail about the Flying area with detailled explanations why 
	- wants to feel like a part of a community
		- by connecting to veteran pilots to get confirmation of their learnings

DCI Member Competitive
	- wants to feel like a part of a community
		- by competing with other Clubs as a DCI Member
		- by flying together with other DCI Members in a Team
		- by beeing seen on top lists published by DCI
		- by beeing singled out for good performances
		- by getting prices and recognition on DCI Events
	

DCI Member Veteran
	- wants to feel like a part of a community
		- by beeing asked for their Insights on the current situations
		- by connecting with fellow veterans regularly

Licensed Paragliders from Outside of the Area
	- wants current and reliable information about Launch Sites 
		- How to get there
		- What to look for
		- what conditions are needed
		- what resources can i use to get to a decision

Paragliding and Hanggliding Newbies
	- Get reliable insights into where to start the journey to become a pilot
	- Get reliable insights what schools are related to DCI
	
Paragliding & Hanggliding Tandem Customers

Paragliding & Hanggliding Tandem Firms
